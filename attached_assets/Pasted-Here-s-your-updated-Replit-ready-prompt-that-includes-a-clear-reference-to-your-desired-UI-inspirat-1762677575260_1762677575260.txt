## 5. API Endpoints & Flows

- **Auth:** `/api/auth/login` (instant sign-in as user/admin), toggle in UI.
- **Blogs:** `/api/blogs`, `/api/blogs/:id`, `/api/blogs/featured`,  
  `/api/blogs/:id/related` (Milvus-based semantic and tag/category similarity)
- **Categories/Tags:** `/api/categories`, `/api/categories/:name`, `/api/tags`
- **Search:** `/api/search?q=term` (text + vector)
- **Chatbot:** `/api/ai/chat` `{query, blog_id}` (GROQ Q&A/summarization/comparison)
- **Admin CRUD:** expedited for blogs, categories, tags when admin mode active.
- **Homepage:** aggregates featured/latest posts for carousel/grid as in inspiration sites.
